About Us

Myanmar Space Exploration is at the forefront of technological innovation, dedicated to pushing the boundaries of space exploration. Our mission is to inspire, educate, and pave the way for future generations to reach for the stars.

With cutting-edge technology and a team of passionate individuals, we aim to contribute to humanity's understanding of the cosmos. Join us in this exciting journey as we explore new frontiers and make history in the realm of space.

Our Missions

At Myanmar Space Exploration, we embark on a variety of missions with the goal of advancing our understanding of the universe. Our missions range from satellite launches and space probes to collaborative projects with international space agencies.

One of our flagship missions includes the establishment of a lunar research station to conduct experiments and gather valuable data about the Moon. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of space exploration for the benefit of all mankind.
