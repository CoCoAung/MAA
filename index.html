About MAEU

Located in Meiktila, the Myanmar Aerospace Engineering University (MAEU) was founded in 2002. It serves as the primary hub for cultivating specialized human resources in the field of aerospace technology.

MAEU offers undergraduate and graduate programs designed to equip students with technical expertise in aircraft design, satellite systems, and propulsion technologies, fostering the next generation of Myanmar's engineers.
